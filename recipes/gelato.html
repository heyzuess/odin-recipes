Gelato Recipe

Description

Gelato is a easy recipe that can be set and forgotten. It does not take a lot of ingredients to make and can be pretty cheap.

Originally a dish from Italy, Gelato has become a staple of american cuisne where multiple variations of the dish can be found across the United States.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sandwich Bags (Small and Large)
  • Ice
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate Syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Add milk, sugar, vanilla in a sandwich bag and seal it tightly
  2. In a different bag add salt and ice
  3. Place the small bag inside of the big bag
  4. Shake bag as hard as you can for 5 minutes
  5. Don't forget to dance... it gives it a little bit of flavor
  6. Remove small bag and rinse the outside and opening to remove excess salt
