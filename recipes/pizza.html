Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a easy recipe that can be set and forgotten. It does not take a lot of ingredients to make and can be pretty cheap.

Originally a dish from Italy, Pizza has become a staple of american cuisne where multiple variations of the dish can be found across the United States.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepperoni (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Shred cheese if needed
  3. Heat up sauce in a seperate sauce pan
  4. Don't forget to dance... it gives it a little bit of flavor
  5. Get out a baking tin of some sort to hold ingredients
