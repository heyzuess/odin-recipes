Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a easy recipe that can be set and forgotten. It does not take a lot of ingredients to make and can be pretty cheap.

Originally a dish from Italy, Lasagna has become a staple of american cuisne where multiple variations of the dish can be found across the United States.
